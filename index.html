Biography

My name is Alee, and I have always been driven by a deep curiosity for how things work, especially in the realm of technology. Growing up, I was the type of person who loved taking apart gadgets, whether it was an old mobile phone, a radio, or even a computer. My fascination with the inner workings of machines led me to pursue a formal education in technology and engineering. I earned a degree in Engineering from O.A.U, where I gained a solid foundation in programming, systems engineering, and software development.

During my time at university, I worked on several projects that honed my skills in various areas of engineering. Whether it was developing apps, automating processes, or collaborating with teams on hardware-software integration projects, I quickly realized that my passion for technology extended beyond just theory—it was the ability to use this knowledge to solve real-world problems that truly inspired me. When I am not immersed in the world of code and circuits, I enjoy reading, wating movies, and playing table tennis. These activities help me stay grounded and remind me that creativity is not only for artists—engineers and tech enthusiasts need it too, to think outside the box and innovate.

Why I joined AltSchool

I joined AltSchool because I believe it offers a unique, immersive learning experience that goes beyond conventional education models. AltSchool's focus on project-based learning, where students actively engage with real-world challenges, is aligned with my personal philosophy of learning by doing. This model allows me to apply theoretical knowledge to practical situations, accelerating my growth as an engineer.

Additionally, AltSchool’s reputation for fostering a culture of collaboration and mentorship was a significant factor in my decision. The opportunity to work alongside a community of like-minded individuals, all passionate about technology and innovation, is invaluable. I also appreciate that AltSchool is deeply committed to preparing students for the ever-changing demands of the tech industry by emphasizing cutting-edge skills like AI, cloud computing, and advanced software development practices

Furthermore, the flexibility of the School of Engineering Program at AltSchool allows for personalization, so I can focus on areas I am passionate about while exploring emerging technologies. This flexibility, combined with a strong network of industry professionals, will enable me to grow faster and be better prepared for the challenges of the modern tech landscape.

Goals for the School of Engineering Program

My primary goal for joining the School of Engineering Program at AltSchool is to deepen my technical expertise and become proficient in advanced software engineering methodologies. I want to build on my existing knowledge, but I am particularly excited about exploring new areas, such as artificial intelligence, blockchain technology, and cloud infrastructure. These technologies are shaping the future, and I aim to be at the forefront of their development and implementation.

Another goal is to work on large-scale projects that have real-world applications, which is something I believe the program will offer. By working on these projects, I hope to enhance my problem-solving skills, gain experience in managing complex engineering processes, and learn how to deliver scalable and efficient solutions in a collaborative environment.

I am also looking to expand my professional network through the program, connecting with both my peers and mentors. Building these relationships will not only help me learn from others but also open doors to future career opportunities. I aspire to one day contribute to impactful projects in areas such as fintech, healthcare technology, or even education, and I believe that AltSchool will provide me with the tools and connections to make that dream a reality.

Finally, my personal mission is to use technology to improve lives, whether by increasing efficiency in systems or by developing solutions that address critical societal challenges. The School of Engineering Program at AltSchool offers a platform where I can align my technical pursuits with my desire to make a difference, empowering me to grow both personally and professionally.
